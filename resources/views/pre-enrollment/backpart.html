B. Policy on withdrawals and refund

The date of filling the notice or withdrawal shall be considered the date of withdrawal and shall be the basis in determining the amount to be charged for the term. Computation for adjustment will be in the accounting Office.

1. Enrollment deposited can be refunded subject to the following conditions.

a. 80% refund before the subject are encoded and before the enrollment closes.

b. 20% refund if the subject are encoded before the enrollment closes.

c. No refund after the enrollment closes.

2. 10% charge on tuition and other school fees if a student withdraws within the first week of classes regardless of whether or not
he/she actually attended classes.

3. 20% charge on tuition and other school fees if a student withdraws within the first week of classes regardless of whether or not
he/she actually attended classes.

4. 50% charge on tuition and other school fees if a student withdraws within the first week of classes regardless of whether or not
he/she actually attended classes.

5. 75% charge on tuition and other school fees if a student withdraws within the first week of classes regardless of whether or not
he/she actually attended classes.

6. 100% charge on tuition and other school fees if a student withdraws within the first week of classes regardless of whether or not
he/she actually attended classes.
